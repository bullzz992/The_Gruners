This application is used to detect vehicle number plates and retrieve further information related to the vehicle.

Access needed

  • Camera
  • Internet Connection
  • Location

Targets

Vehicle Number Plates

Instructions

  • Point camera at target to detect
  • Tap and drag to distort video background
  • Double tap to access options menu
  • Tap on a detected Number Plate to view detection results
